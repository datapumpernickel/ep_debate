Verbatim report of proceedings
Thursday, 16 September 2004 - Strasbourg OJ edition

8. Vote
 		 

  President. We shall now proceed to the vote on:

Joint motion for a resolution(1) on the situation in Myanmar/ASEM

(Parliament adopted the joint resolution)

Joint motion for a resolution(2) on the harassment of minorities in Vojvodina

(Parliament adopted the joint resolution)

Joint motion for a resolution(3) on the political situation in the Maldives

(Parliament adopted the joint resolution)

 
  

(1) Tabled by Geoffrey Van Orden, Charles Tannock, Nirj Deva and Bernd Posselt, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, Glenys Kinnock, Mauro Zani, Margrietus J. van den Berg and Pasqualina Napoletano, on behalf of the PSE Group, Johan Van Hecke, on behalf of the ALDE/ADLE Group, Frithjof Schmidt, on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group and Vittorio Emanuele Agnoletto, on behalf of the GUE/NGL Group, seeking to replace motions for resolutions B6-0046/2004, B6-0048/2004, B6-0056/2004, B6-0059/2004 and B6-0061/2004 with a new text.
(2) Tabled by Kinga Gál, Doris Pack, Péter Olajos and Bernd Posselt, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, Véronique De Keyser, Johannes (Hannes) Swoboda, Jan Marinus Wiersma, Csaba Sándor Tabajdi and Pasqualina Napoletano, on behalf of the PSE Group, István Szent-Iványi and Jelko Kacin, on behalf of the ALDE/ADLE Group, Joost Lagendijk, Gisela Kallenbach, Tatjana Ždanoka and Raül Romeva Rueda, on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, Roberto Musacchio, on behalf of the GUE/NGL Group, Bastiaan Belder, on behalf of the IND/DEM Group and Adriana Poli Bortone, on behalf of the UEN Group, seeking to replace motions for resolutions B6-0045/2004, B6-0049/2004, B6-0055/2004, B6-0057/2004, B6-0060/2004, B6-0062/2004 and B6-0064/2004 with a new text.
(3) Tabled by Nirj Deva, Charles Tannock, Geoffrey Van Orden and Thomas Mann, on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, Pasqualina Napoletano, on behalf of the PSE Group, Graham R. Watson, on behalf of the ALDE/ADLE Group and Cristiana Muscardini, on behalf of the UEN Group, seeking to replace motions for resolutions B6-0044/2004, B6-0054/2004, B6-0058/2004 and B6-0063/2004 with a new text.
