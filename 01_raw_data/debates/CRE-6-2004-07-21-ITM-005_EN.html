Verbatim report of proceedings
Wednesday, 21 July 2004 - Strasbourg OJ edition

5. Election of Quaestors
 		 

  Poettering (PPE-DE). – (DE) Mr President, ladies and gentlemen, I think it is important to note that our group has two candidates for the office of Quaestor, namely Mr James Nicholson and Mrs Godelieve Quisthoudt-Rowohl. Neither the Bureau nor the chairman of the group are supporting any other candidates.

 
  
 		 

  President. It shall appear thus in the Minutes.

 
  
 		 

  President. The next item is the election of Quaestors, which could not take place before the midday break.

I shall repeat the list of candidates:

for the PPE-DE Group, Mr James Nicholson and Mrs Godelieve Quisthoudt-Rowohl,

for the PSE Group, Mrs Mia De Vits and Mrs Genowefa Grabowska,

for the ALDE Group, Mrs Anneli Jäätteenmaki,

and, presented by 46 Members, Mrs Astrid Lulling.

Since we still have more candidates than posts to cover, we must vote in accordance with the Rules of Procedure in secret and we will do so, in accordance with the rules applicable to the election of Vice-Presidents, by means of the electronic procedure.

(In successive votes the following were elected Quaestors of the European Parliament:

James Nicholson

Genowefa Grabowska

Mia De Vits

Godelieve Quisthoudt-Rowohl

Astrid Lulling) (1)

 
  

(1) Decision on the numerical composition of committees: see Minutes.
