Verbatim report of proceedings
Wednesday, 17 November 2004 - Strasbourg OJ edition

5. Voting time
 		 

  President. The next item is the vote.

Report (A6-0007/2004) by Joseph Daul, on behalf of the Committee on Agriculture, on the proposal for a Council directive amending Directives 66/401/EEC, 66/402/EEC, 2002/54/EC and 2002/57/EC as regards examinations carried out under official supervision and equivalence of seed produced in third countries

(Parliament adopted the text)

Report (A6-0017/2004) by Jean-Paul Gauzès, on behalf of the Committee on Economic and Monetary Affairs, on the proposal for a Council decision on the conclusion of the Agreement between the European Community and the Principality of Andorra providing for measures equivalent to those laid down in Council Directive 2003/48/EC on taxation of savings income in the form of interest payments

(Parliament adopted the text)

Report (A6-0016/2004) by Jean-Paul Gauzès, on behalf of the Committee on Economic and Monetary Affairs, on the proposal for a Council decision on the conclusion of the Agreement between the European Community and the Principality of Liechtenstein providing for measures equivalent to those laid down in Council Directive 2003/48/EC of 3 June 2003 on taxation of savings income in the form of interest payments

(Parliament adopted the text)

Report (A6-0019/2004) by Martine Roure, on behalf of the Committee on Civil Liberties, Justice and Home Affairs, on the Commission proposal for a Council decision amending Decision 2002/463/EC adopting an action programme for administrative cooperation in the fields of external borders, visas, asylum and immigration (ARGO programme)

(Parliament adopted the text)

Report (A6-0027/2004) by Dorette Corbey, on behalf of the Committee on the Environment, Public Health and Food Safety, on the proposal for a directive of the European Parliament and of the Council amending Directive 94/62/EC on packaging and packaging waste

(Parliament adopted the legislative resolution)

Report (A6-0031/2004) by Anders Samuelsen, on behalf of the Committee on Foreign Affairs, on the proposal for a Council regulation amending Council Regulation (EC) No 2667/2000 on the European Agency for Reconstruction

(Parliament adopted the legislative resolution)

Report (A6-0032/2004) by Mechtild Rothe, on behalf of the Committee on Foreign Affairs, on the proposal for a Council regulation establishing an instrument of financial support for encouraging the economic development of the Turkish Cypriot community

(Parliament adopted the legislative resolution)

Report (A6-0022/2004) by Raül Romeva i Rueda, on behalf of the Committee on Foreign Affairs, on the Council's Fifth Annual Report according to Operative Provision 8 of the European Union Code of Conduct on Arms Exports

(Parliament adopted the resolution)

Motion for a resolution (B6-0162/2004) tabled by the Committee on Foreign Affairs on the European Union military operation 'Althea' in Bosnia and Herzegovina

(Parliament adopted the resolution)

Motion for a resolution (B6-0129/2004) tabled by the Committee on the Environment, Public Health and Food Safety on the EU strategy for the Buenos Aires Conference on Climate Change (COP-10)

(Parliament adopted the resolution)

Joint motion for a resolution(1) on Cuba (RC-B6-0155/2004)

(Parliament rejected the joint motion for a resolution)

 
  
  

Motion for a resolution (B6-0155/2004) tabled by the PSE Group on Cuba

(Parliament rejected the motion for a resolution)

Motion for a resolution (B6-0156/2004) tabled by the PPE-DE, ALDE and UEN Groups on Cuba

Salafranca Sánchez-Neyra (PPE-DE). – (ES) Mr President, I would like to point out that there has been an error by the services of the House and that this amendment has not been presented by the PPE-DE Group. Naturally, we are against.

President. – Thank you for pointing that out, Mr Salafranca. I note that it is not a PPE-DE Group amendment.

(Parliament adopted the resolution)

⁂

Motion for a resolution (B6-0153/2004) tabled by the GUE/NGL Group on the European Council meeting (Brussels, 4-5 November 2004)

(Parliament rejected the motion for a resolution)

Joint motion for a resolution(2) on the outcome of the European Council meeting held in Brussels on 4-5 November 2004.

(Parliament adopted the joint resolution)

⁂

President. - That concludes the vote.

 
  

(1) Tabled by the following Members: Miguel Angel Martínez Martínez, on behalf of the PSE Group, Daniel Marc Cohn-Bendit and others on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, Francis Wurtz and others on behalf of the GUE/NGL Group, seeking to replace motions for resolutions B6-0155 and 0163/2004 with a new text.
(2) Tabled by the following Members: Martin Schulz and Hannes Swoboda, on behalf of the PSE Group, Annemie Neyts-Uyttebroeck and Alexander Nuno Alvaro, on behalf of the ALDE Group, Hans-Gert Poettering and others on behalf of the PPE-DE Group, Daniel Marc Cohn-Bendit and others on behalf of the Verts/ALE Group, Cristiana Muscardini and Anna Elzbieta Fotyga, on behalf of the UEN Group, seeking to replace motions for resolutions B6-0154, 0157, 0158, 0159 and 0161/2004 with a new text.
