Verbatim report of proceedings
Thursday, 14 January 1999 - Strasbourg OJ edition

1. Approval of the Minutes

  President . – The Minutes of yesterday's sitting have been distributed.

Are there any comments?

  Wijsenbeek (ELDR). – (NL) Madam President, I would like to point out with regard to yesterday"s Minutes that for the umpteenth time the UPE Group tabled an oral question with debate, to be moved by Mr Donnay, with an associated resolution on postal services being tabled by Mr Pasty. Yesterday evening at 11.45 p.m. the UPE Group did not even take the trouble to be here – not a single one of them turned up. I would urge that these sorts of unnecessary requests should not be allowed in future, because no new information or views were put forward and the Commission had absolutely nothing to tell us that it had not already told us before.

  President . – We take note of that.

(The Minutes were approved)
