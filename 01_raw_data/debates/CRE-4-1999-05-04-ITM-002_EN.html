Verbatim report of proceedings
Tuesday, 4 May 1999 - Strasbourg OJ edition

2. Decision on urgent procedure

Proposal for a Council Regulation amending Regulation (EC) No 1868/94 establishing a quota system for the production of potato starch (COM(99)0173 – C4‐0214/99‐99/0088(CNS))

  President . – Mr Colino has the floor to give the opinion of the Committee on Agriculture, which is the committee responsible.

  Colino Salamanca (PSE), chairman of the Committee on Agriculture and Rural Development. – (ES) Mr President, this issue was dealt with at the latest meeting of the Committee on Agriculture. It is indeed linked to Agenda 2000 but in our view the Council"s request for urgent procedure is not justified, as the system is only due to come into force in the year 2000. There will therefore be plenty of time for the new Parliament to consider it.

  President . – Thank you, Mr Colino.

Does anyone wish to speak in favour of the Council's proposal? Mr Mulder has the floor.

  Mulder (ELDR). – (NL) Mr President, I would prefer us to deal with this proposal now, firstly because we shall be covering all the other proposals under Agenda 2000 this week, so we might as well deal with this one as well. Secondly, the proposal is neutral in budgetary terms – it has no financial implications. So I think we would be better to cover everything to do with Agenda 2000 this week, and I shall therefore be voting in favour of that.

  President . – Thank you, Mr Mulder.

As no one else wishes to speak, I put the request for urgent procedure to the vote.

(Parliament agreed to urgent procedure)

  President . – Since urgent procedure has been approved, this item will be entered on the agenda for Friday, and the deadline for tabling amendments is tomorrow, Wednesday, at 10 a.m.(1)

(1) Topical and urgent debate (motions for resolutions tabled): see Minutes.
