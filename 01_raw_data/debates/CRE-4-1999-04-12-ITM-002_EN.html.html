Verbatim report of proceedings
Monday, 12 April 1999 - Strasbourg OJ edition

2. Statement by the President

  President . – Ladies and gentlemen, a terrible fire broke out in the Mont Blanc tunnel on 24 March killing at least 40 people, some of whom have yet to be identified. The scale of this tragedy caused great dismay not only in France and Italy, but throughout the European Union. Although it occurred some weeks ago, I am sure I am speaking for all of us in expressing our sorrow at these deaths and our sympathy for the victims" families. We hope that the investigations being carried out will prevent such tragedies from occurring in the future.(1)
