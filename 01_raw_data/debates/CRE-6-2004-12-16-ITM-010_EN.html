Verbatim report of proceedings
Thursday, 16 December 2004 - Strasbourg OJ edition

10. Welcome
 		 

  President. – I should like to welcome the delegation from the Assembly of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, chaired by Mr Zaid bin Al-Muhsin Al-Hussein. The importance we attach to this visit, which is the first to the Chamber of the European Parliament since 1995, should be emphasised.

(Applause)

This visit comes at a very significant moment, especially in view of the fact that Saudi Arabia will be holding its first local elections in February 2005. This visit constitutes a new stage in the deepening of relations between the Saudi people and the European Union.

 
  
 		 

  Frassoni (Verts/ALE). – (IT) Mr President, we are very pleased about the holding of local elections in Saudi Arabia, but very much deplore the lack of participation by women.

(Applause)

 
  
  

(The sitting was suspended at 12.35 p.m. and resumed at 3 p.m.)

 
  
  

IN THE CHAIR: MR VIDAL-QUADRAS ROCA
Vice-President
